HRIDAY

Derived from Sanskrit word "HRIDAY" means "heart, center of anything,
mind, soul”. We put our core values of Humanity, Responsiveness,
Innovation, Determination, Attitude and Yog at the heart of
everything we do and are central to our work approach.





HUMANITY

Humanity is the glorious value which we believe in and inculcates in our work environment.
It is the value of compassion, kindness and consideration for other. We believe in the philosophy of “Vasudev Katumbhakabh”(Universe is One),environment free from biased behavior ,favoritism and treating everyone including peers ,teammates, suboratinates and professional relations equally and fairly.

RESPONSIVENESS

Level of responsiveness in serving customers can be evaluated from three perspectives: speed of service, sensitivity to customer concerns and awareness of changes in the general needs of our customer.
We exhibit responsiveness in our willingness to help customers and to provide prompt service in a dependable and accurate manner

INNOVATION

We at Jivinc promote an innovative approach and applying innovative thinking and creativity to everything.
We aim at exploring new ideas, applications, designs and services.

DETERMINATION

We at JIVINC are passionate about the work and duties which we undertake.
We at have dedication and passion to serve our clients, passion to realize our vision and mission and serve society at large.

ATTITUDE

We believe in the power of positive attitude and possess the courage to bring a positive change and also inspire others. As right put by Lu Holtz “Ability is what you are capable of doing. Motivation determines what you do. Attitude determines how well you do it.

YOG

The Sanskrit word yoga has the literal meaning of "yoke", from a root yuj meaning to join, to unite, or to attach. We at Jivinc foster teamwork thorough collaborations, integration in our core values, open communication and maintaining a positive attitude reflect our commitment to a synergy based work place.
